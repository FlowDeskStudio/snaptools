About This Tool

A simple, privacy-focused utility to extract text from your PDF files directly into Microsoft Word format.

Privacy First: Most free PDF converters upload your highly sensitive documents to arbitrary third-party servers. Our tool uses WebAssembly, PDF.js, and browser-native Javascript to process the PDF entirely inside your local cache. Your files, documents, and private data never leave your screen.
