Free PDF to Word Text Extractor

Stop manually re-typing text from PDFs. Our free PDF to Word tool is a fast, specialized utility that scans your PDF documents and extracts the raw textual content into editable Microsoft Word (.docx) formats. Designed for speed, privacy, and simplicity.

How to Convert PDF to Word

  1. Drag and drop your PDF documents into the upload box.
  2. Click "Convert to Word" and wait just a few seconds as the text is parsed.
  3. Download the generated `.docx` files to immediately begin editing the text in Microsoft Word, Google Docs, or LibreOffice.

Privacy Focused: No Uploads Required

Most free "PDF Converters" on the web force you to upload your incredibly sensitive files (invoices, contracts, legal documents) to their arbitrary cloud servers. That is a massive security risk. This devsnap.net tool uses PDF.js and WebAssembly to read the PDF and construct the Word file entirely within your local browser's memory. Your business documents are never uploaded anywhere.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Will this preserve my exact layout, tables, and images?

A: No. Because this tool runs free, offline, and in your browser, it is intentionally designed as a Text Extractor. It will rip out the paragraphs and raw text so you don't have to retype them, but complex visual layouts or images will not be faithfully recreated in Word.

Q: Can it convert scanned PDFs (images) to text?

A: This tool is currently limited to PDFs that have actual text layers embedded. It does not perform OCR (Optical Character Recognition) on scanned images or photos of documents.
