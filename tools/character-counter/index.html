About This Tool

This free Character Counter gives you real-time statistics about your text including character count, word count, sentence count, paragraph count, and estimated reading time. It also shows your top keywords for SEO analysis.

Use the preset limits for popular social media platforms like Twitter/X (280 characters), Instagram (2,200), or SEO limits like meta descriptions (160) and title tags (60). Perfect for writers, bloggers, students, and marketers.
