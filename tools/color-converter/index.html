About This Tool

This free Color Converter lets you convert between HEX, RGB, and HSL color formats. Use the sliders or enter a color value to see instant conversions and a live preview. Perfect for web designers and developers.

HEX codes are used in HTML/CSS, RGB is commonly used in design tools, and HSL provides intuitive control over hue, saturation, and lightness. All conversions happen locally in your browser.
