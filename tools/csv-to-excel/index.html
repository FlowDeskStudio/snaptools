Free CSV to Excel Converter (Fixes Garbled Text/文字化け)

This free converter instantly transforms CSV data into native Microsoft Excel (.xlsx) spreadsheets. It is specially designed to automatically detect and fix frustrating character encoding problems like the famous garbled text (文字化け) issue often encountered when Japanese CSV files (Shift-JIS) are opened directly in modern Excel.

How to Convert CSV to Excel

  1. Drag and drop one or multiple `.csv` files into the upload box above.
  2. Click the "Convert to Excel" button.
  3. The system will auto-detect the file's text encoding (such as UTF-8 or Shift-JIS) and instantly generate perfectly formatted `.xlsx` files without garbled text.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Why does Excel show weird alien text when I open a CSV?

A: This happens because Excel expects text in a specific encoding format, but the CSV was saved in another (like Windows Shift-JIS vs UTF-8). Our tool detects the original encoding and translates it correctly into a standard Excel file format.

Q: Can I convert multiple files at once?

A: Yes! You can drag and drop dozens of CSV files into the box. Clicking convert will process and download them all automatically.
