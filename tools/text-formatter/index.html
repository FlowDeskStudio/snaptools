About This Tool

This text formatter helps you quickly clean up messy text copied from PDFs, websites, or rushed meeting notes.

It instantly fixes inconsistent Japanese character widths (Zenkaku/Hankaku rules), removes excessive blank lines and trailing spaces, and standardizes bullet points. Perfect for preparing text before sending business emails or pasting into Word documents. 100% Client-Side: Your text is processed securely in your browser and is never sent to any server.
